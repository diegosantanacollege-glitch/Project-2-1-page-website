What are some warning signs of general anxiety disorder?

Some signs may include:

  • Inability to control intrusive thoughts.
  • Feeling overwhelmed.
  • Chronic fatigue.
  • Worst case thinking.
  • Chronic Insommina/Waking up feeling exhausted.

What are some warning signs of depression?

Some signs may include:

  • Persistant sadness.
  • Physical fatigue.
  • Ideas of self loathing.
  • Irrability
  • Changes in sleep/appetite.

What are some warning signs of suicide?

Some signs may include:

  • Increased thoughts of death.
  • Dramatic mood changes.
  • Giving away prized objects.
  • Withdrawing socially.
  • Increased risk taking.
